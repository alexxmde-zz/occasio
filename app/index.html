Vulnerability analysis has the purpose of discovering flaws in the systems and environments being evaluated. There are several forms of evaluations that vary by scope. These different kinds of evaluations are conducted at a given time, which can span days or even weeks. When the analyzes are done periodically, in this case there is vulnerability management. Through the results obtained in the vulnerability analysis companies can protect against future attacks.

Vulnerability management is a service that includes ongoing vulnerability analysis performed at periodic intervals rather than sporadically. In certain cases, this time interval is "continuous", that is, the evaluation is done all the time in an automated way. Conducting periodic tests, so that the target environment can be validated, will allow monitoring the evolution of environmental safety to the timeframe and evaluate its improvement

Risk Management

Risk is the combination of a particular event to happen and the impact to be caused. An event is the relationship between the threats, the vulnerabilities and the damage caused. The Risk Management Process in Information Security are coordinated activities to direct and control an organization with regard to risks. It is appropriate that risks are identified and qualified, prioritized according to the risk assessment criteria and the relevant objectives of the organization.

Stages of risk analysis:

  • Identificação dos recursos considerados críticos para a organização.
  • Identificação das vulnerabilidades que se encontram associadas a estes recursos e das ameaças a que estes se encontram expostos.
  • Determinação, o mais realista possível, das perdas e danos (tangíveis e intangíveis) associados aos impactos resultantes da concretização de uma ou mais ameaças, sobre um dado recurso.
  • De acordo com o nível de risco identificado, segue-se a classificação deste quanto à sua aceitação ou necessidade de mitigação.
  • Um nível de risco pode ser aceito caso a organização decida que este não acarreta conseqüências significativas para a concretização das suas atividades críticas de negócio.

PCI Compliance

PCI Compliance or "PCI DSS" is the Data Security Standard for the Payment Card Industry. This certification is required for all companies that process and transmit card data over the internet. The certification process encompasses strict security requirements such as several security tests, such as vulnerability analysis and intrusion testing, periodically made to detect system weaknesses.

Forensic Computing

It is the collection, retrieval, data analysis that aims to reconstruct the course of actions and recreate authentic complete scenarios. Forensic computing aims to obtain information by analyzing information from a computer, network, or data storage device that is the subject of investigation for cyber crimes. It is widely used to uncover virtual crimes, identity theft, financial crimes, using special techniques to track information.

DDOS and Stress Testing

"Stress Testing" is the process of determining the ability of a computer, network, program or device to maintain a certain level of effectiveness under unfavorable conditions. The process may involve quantitative tests done in a laboratory, such as measuring the frequency of errors or system failures. The term also refers to the qualitative assessment of factors such as availability or resistance to denial of service (DOS) attacks or denial of service attacks (DDOS).
